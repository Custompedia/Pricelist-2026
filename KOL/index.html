Campaign Lead Time
Minimum 5 business days from confirmed booking to first KOL posting date. For campaigns involving 50 or more KOL, allow 7–10 business days to complete vetting, dealing, and contract signing. Rush campaigns are available with a surcharge — subject to team availability.
📦
Product Seeding — Shipping Handled by Client
For affiliate or seeding campaigns, product delivery to each KOL is handled directly by the client. Custompedia compiles a complete, organized KOL address list — name, address, and phone number — formatted and ready for your shipping team or courier. Campaign timeline begins upon confirmation of product receipt by each KOL, not from the shipping date. Client is responsible for ensuring timely delivery.

KOL Replacement — Included, No Extra Cost
If any KOL drops out or becomes unavailable for any reason, Custompedia replaces them at no additional charge — up to 3 replacement slots per campaign. We vetted them, we recommended them: the accountability is ours. Replacement KOL passes the same 5-step vetting. Replacement does not reset campaign timeline or period.
©️
Content Usage Rights
KOL content usage rights — including reposts, whitelisting, and use in ads — are specified in the contract drafted by Custompedia. Scope and duration are defined per campaign. Extended rights beyond the standard scope can be negotiated prior to contract signing.
⚠️
What Is Not Included
KOL fees and talent payments · Product costs and shipping · Platform tools or third-party analytics subscriptions · Ads spend for Spark Ads or Partnership Ads · Premium or celebrity KOL rates (custom quote required) · Any guaranteed outcome on organic reach, engagement, or sales.
📋
No Organic Performance Guarantee
KOL Management is a fully managed service. Custompedia does not guarantee specific reach, engagement, or sales results from organic KOL activity. The Performance-Based pricing model (Section 04) creates a shared incentive structure for affiliate campaigns. Organic outcomes depend on KOL quality, content execution, and timing — all of which Custompedia optimizes, but does not control.
💳
Payment Terms
Per campaign: 50% deposit upon booking confirmation, 50% due upon campaign launch. Retainer: monthly invoicing, due within 7 days of invoice date. KOL fees are invoiced separately — paid to Custompedia, which disburses to each KOL directly. No campaign is activated until the deposit is confirmed.

Confidentiality
All campaign briefs, budget details, and KOL selection data are handled confidentially. An NDA is available upon request for clients with sensitive product information or unreleased campaign materials.
